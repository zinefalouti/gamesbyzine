    Trying To Make Games

    A 2014 software engineer graduate and academic, my passion for game development began much earlier during my second year of high school when I started creating 2D HTML5 side-scrolling web games. In 2017, I embarked on my first major project, The Stones of Yalmrith. This learning experience not only deepened my understanding of the game development industry and its processes but also allowed me to refine my skills in areas such as sculpting, retopology, baking, score composition, and environment design. As a lifelong gamer, I grew up with classic consoles from the pre-3D era, which instilled in me a deep appreciation for games and the talented people who create them. If you'd like to learn more about my work, collaborate on a project, or simply have an old drinking chat, feel free to reach out—I’d be happy to connect!
